mongodb commands: 
1. insert one ---> db.coll_name.insertOne()
2. insert many ---> db.coll_name.insertMany()
3. find one ----------> db.coll_name.findOne()
4. find Many ----------> db.coll_name.findMany()
5. update one ----------> db.coll_name.updateOne()
6. update Many ----------> db.coll_name.updateMany()
7. delete one ----------> db.coll_name.deleteOne()
8. delete Many ----------> db.coll_name.deleteMany()
